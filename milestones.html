The second progress presentation covered the completed system implementations and empirical evaluation results. Notable milestones included the migration to Gemini 2.0 Flash Native Speech API (C1), completion of the 48-participant biometric dataset collection (C4), and full route optimizer with weather sampling validation (C3).

🎙️C1: Gemini Native Speech API integration achieving 2.1s latency and 4.7 MOS
🔐C2: Complete 7-step pipeline with ensemble fusion and graceful degradation
🗺️C3: Route optimizer with weather severity circles and priority mode validated
👁️C4: 48-participant dataset collected across 4 capture scenarios; FAR 2.1% achieved
📊Ablation studies and comparative evaluation results

Marks15%
TypeOral Exam

The viva voce is the final oral examination in which each student defends their individual research component before a panel of examiners. Students are expected to demonstrate a thorough understanding of their chosen methodology, explain all design decisions, and respond to technical questions about their implementation and evaluation approach.

🎤Individual oral defence of research component
🔬Technical questioning on methodology, results, and limitations
💬Platform-level integration discussion
📋Examiner feedback and final marks awarded
