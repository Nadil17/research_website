About Us

Four final year information technology students at SLIIT collaborating on an intelligent logistics platform that combines AI research with practical engineering.

👥

Our Team

Project members and responsibilities

The project is developed by a four-member team under the B.Sc. (Hons) in Information Technology programme at SLIIT. Each member contributes to a distinct component while maintaining a shared research and implementation workflow.

Goal: build a unified courier intelligence platform with voice AI, secure verification, route optimization, and multimodal authentication.

🎓

Project Context

SLIIT group research project

The platform addresses courier logistics challenges through applied AI and modern web systems, with a focus on last-mile delivery, identity verification, and customer accessibility.
